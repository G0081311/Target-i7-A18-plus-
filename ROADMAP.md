## 🚨 Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| **Payment delays** | Revenue stops | Have cash reserves, 2nd payment gateway (Flutterwave) |
| **User growth plateaus** | Revenue flat | Pivot marketing, add features, target new segments |
| **Churn (people leave)** | Negative growth | Improve onboarding, community, support quality |
| **Feature bloat** | Complexity kills product | Strict roadmap discipline, say "no" to features |
| **Competition** | Market share loss | Move fast, focus on Ogoja niche, build moat |
| **Government changes** | Regulatory issues | Build relationships, legal team, compliance first |
| **Key person risk** (Michael) | Operations collapse | Document everything, train team, AI agents handle ops |
| **Technical debt** | Slow iterations | Refactor quarterly, clean codebase |

## ✨ Core Promises

Whatever happens in development:

1. **Always put users first** — features serve real needs
2. **Keep it simple** — complexity = product death
3. **Move fast** — weekly releases, not yearly
4. **Be honest** — communicate delays, failures, learnings
5. **Serve Ogoja** — never forget where we started
6. **Use AI right** — technology serves humans, not vice versa
7. **Open doors** — partnerships over competition
